Cauliflower mash

Cheesy, Gluten Free, keto, Sides, Starch, Vegetarian

Ingredients

  • 1 head cauliflower
  • 3 tbsp butter
  • 3 cloves garlic, smashed
  • thyme
  • 1 tbsp cream cheese
  • 1 oz heavy cream
  • 1/4 to 1/2 cup shredded Parmesan

Directions

Break off florets of cauliflower and steam for 10-15 minutes

Melt butter in small saucepan over medium heat. Add smashed garlic and sautee for 2-3 minutes

Puree cauliflower, butter, garlic, thyme, cream cheese, cream, and Parmesan in food processor until smooth
