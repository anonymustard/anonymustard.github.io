4 Ingredient Crock Pot Chocolate Peanut Clusters

chocolate, cookies, Dessert, Holiday, nuts

Prep 5 minutes Cook 2 hours Makes 72 (Scaled) Source 101cookingfortwo.com

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 pounds dry roasted peanuts salted or light salted
  • 1 1/2 pounds white almond bark
  • 3/4 pound semi-sweet chocolate chips or chunks
  • 3 ozs German milk chocolate bar

Directions

Dump the peanuts into a medium to large crock pot. Top with the rest of the ingredients. Cover and cook on low for 2 hours.

Mix well. Spoon heaping spoonfuls onto parchment paper. Try not to make them too big. It is candy not cookies.

Allow to cool for about 2 hours.

Notes

2016-12-26 JAD:

Buy Almond Bark at Wal-Mart

This recipe is enormously flexible. Can substitute almonds, cashews, walnuts, or pretzels instead of peanuts. Switch out the almond bark flavor or use white chocolate.

No need for slow cooker: Melt several batches in large bowls at one time in oven set to 200F or Warm for about 45 minutes.

Served for XMas 2016:

"Almond Joy": almonds+ coconut flakes + chocolate and vanilla almond bark + semi-sweet chips + milk chocolate

"Turtle": peanuts + caramel chunks + chocolate almond bark + semi-sweet chips + milk chocolate (add caramel after melting chocolate)

cashews + white almond bark + white chocolate chips + white chocolate bar

pretzel sticks + slivered almonds + chocolate almond bark + semi-sweet chips + milk chocolate
