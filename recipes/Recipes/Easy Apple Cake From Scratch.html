Easy Apple Cake From Scratch

Dessert, Gluten Free

Prep 10 minutes Cook 45 minutes Makes 8 Source 101cookingfortwo.com

Ingredients

  • 2 apples medium or large, shredded or diced
  • 72.5 grams white rice flour
  • 22.6 grams brown rice flour
  • 21.1 grams potato starch
  • 9.1 grams tapioca starch
  • 2.2 grams powdered milk
  • 1 tsp xanthan gum
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 218 grams oil
  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 cup walnuts chopped
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoons salt
  • 1/2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 1 tablespoon sugar optional for sprinkling on top

Directions

Preheat oven to 350 with the rack in the middle. Prep a 9-inch round cake pan with butter and flour.

Dice or shred two medium apples.

Mix all ingredients well and move to prepared pan.

Sprinkle with about a tablespoon of sugar.

Bake until toothpick comes out clean. About 45 minutes. Cool before serving.
