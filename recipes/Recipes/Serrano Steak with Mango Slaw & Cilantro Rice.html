Serrano Steak with Mango Slaw & Cilantro Rice

Beef, Dinner, Full meal, Gluten Free, Meat

Prep 10 Cook 30 Makes 2 Source Hello Fresh

Ingredients

  • 12 ounces flank steak
  • 1 mango, cut into thin matchsticks
  • 1/2 red onion, sliced
  • 1/2 red onion, diced
  • 1 lime, zest and juice
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 3/4 cup basmati rice
  • 1 bunches cilantro, leaves and stems separated, chopped
  • 1 serrano peppers, minced
  • 2 tablespoons balsamic vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil

Directions

In a small pot, heat ½ tablespoon olive oil over medium heat. Add the diced onion and cook, tossing, 2-3 minutes, until softened. Add the rice to the pot and toss to coat, then add 1 ½ cup water and a large pinch of salt to the pot. Bring to a boil, cover, and reduce to a simmer for 15 minutes, until rice is tender.

Make the marinade: Combine 1 1/2 tablespoons olive oil, balsamic vinegar, garlic, half of the minced serrano pepper, salt, and pepper. Add the steak to the marinade and flip to coat. Season with salt and pepper.

Make the mango slaw: toss mango matchsticks, cilantro stems, sliced onions, remaining serranos, lime juice, and half the lime zest.

Heat a large pan over medium-high heat. Add the steak, and cook for 4-5 minutes per side. Rest for 5 minutes, and then slice against the grain.

Stir the cilantro leams and half the lime zest into the rice.

Plate the rice, then top with sliced steak and mango slaw.
