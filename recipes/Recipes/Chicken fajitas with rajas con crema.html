Chicken fajitas with rajas con crema

"Staples", Chicken, Full meal, Gluten Free, Tex-Mex

Prep 45 minutes Cook 15 minutes Source Adapted from Cook's Illustrated

Ingredients

  • Marinated chicken:
  • 4 tbsp vegetable oil, separated
  • 2 tbsp lime juice
  • 4 cloves garlic, peeled and smashed
  • 1 1/2 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp ground cumin
  • 1/2 tsp ground black pepper
  • 1/4 tsp cayenne pepper
  • 1 1/2 lbs boneless skinless chicken breasts, pounded to 1/2 inch thickness

  • Rajas con crema:
  • 1 lb poblano chiles, stemmed, halved, and seeded
  • 1 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1 onion, halved and sliced 1/4 inch thick
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 tsp dried thyme
  • 1/4 tsp dried oregano
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream or Mexican crema
  • 1 tbsp lime juice (if using heavy cream)
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp pepper

  • Other fajitas vegetables (optional):
  • Red onion
  • Red bell pepper
  • Green bell pepper
  • Jalapenos
  • vegetable oil
  • Salt and pepper

  • To serve:
  • 6-inch tortillas
  • minced cilantro
  • lime wedges
  • sour cream
  • salsa
  • cheese

Directions

Chicken:

Whisk 3 tbsp oil, lime juice, garlic, paprika, sugar, salt, cumin, pepper, and cayenne. Add chicken and toss to coat. Cover and let stand for at least 30 minutes

Heat remaining 1 tbsp oil over medium high heat. Add chicken and cook until bottom side is well charred, about 4-5 minutes. Flip chicken and cook until seared and chicken is cooked through. Keep warm.

Rajas con crema:

Set oven rack to highest position and turn on broiler. Place poblanos skin side up on a baking sheet and press down to flatten. Broil until skin is charred, 5-10 minutes, rotating once. Move poblanos to a bowl and cover with plastic wrap. Let rest for 10 minutes in covered bowl. Remove poblanos from bowl and peel off skin. Slice peppers into 1/4 inch thick strips.

Heat oil in a large skillet over medium high heat. Add onion and cook until charred and softened, 5 minutes. Add cream and stir until onion is coated, about 1-2 minutes. Add poblanos, lime juice (if using), salt and pepper and toss to coat. Set aside and keep warm.

Other fajitas vegetables:

Heat oil in a cast iron skilled over medium high or high heat. Add desired vegetables, salt and pepper, and toss to coat with oil. Sautee vegetables stirring constantly until cooked through and charred.

To serve:

Slice chicken breasts into thin strips. Place on tortillas and top with Rajas or vegetables and any other toppings.
