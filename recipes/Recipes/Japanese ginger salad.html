Japanese ginger salad

"Staples", keto

Ingredients

  • Dressing:
  • 35 grams onions, chopped
  • 4 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 3 tbsp rice vinegar
  • 28 grams ginger root, minced
  • 1 clove garlic
  • 1 tbsp ketchup
  • 1 packet splenda or 1 tsp sugar
  • 25 grams white (shiro) miso
  • 30 grams radish, chopped
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil

  • Salad:
  • 3 ounces lettuce
  • 1 ounce cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 ounce radish, halved and sliced thin
  • 1 ounce cucumber, halved and sliced thin

Directions

Dressing: Combine all ingredients in food processor and blend until vegetables are finely minced
