Good Spiced Thin Cut Pork Chops

"Staples", Gluten Free, keto, Meat

Ingredients

  • Brine:
  • 33 grams kosher salt or granulated salt
  • 25 grams granulated sugar or brown sugar
  • 1 quart water

  • 2 8 ounce thin pork chops
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp pepper
  • 1/2 tsp sugar
  • 1/4 tsp cinnamon
  • 1/4 tsp cumin
  • 1/16 tsp ground cloves
  • 1 tbsp oil

Directions

Brine chops for at least 30 minutes. Pat dry.

Slice fat layer of chops every 1-2 inches. Cover both sides of chops with seasoning mixture.

Heat oil on medium high heat in cast iron skillet. Sear chops about 2-3 minutes per side until done.
