Turkey Meatballs w/ spinach butter

Gluten Free, keto, Meat

Source Ruled.me

Ingredients

  • Meatballs:
  • 10 slices bacon
  • 2 pounds ground turkey
  • 3 small red chilies, diced
  • ½ medium green pepper, diced
  • 1 small onion, diced
  • 1 ounce pork rinds, crushed
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ½ teaspoon pepper
  • 1 large handful spinach
  • 3 sprigs thyme
  • 2 large eggs

  • Spinach "butter":
  • 1 large handful spinach
  • reserved bacon grease
  • Seasonings



Directions

Line a baking sheet with foil and add your bacon. Preheat the oven to 400F.

Once the oven is heated, add bacon to oven and cook for 30 minutes or until crisp.

While bacon is cooking, process pork rinds. Then process 1 handful of spinach with thyme.

Add chilies, peppers, onion, pork rinds, salt, pepper, and processed spinach to turkey. Mix with hands.

Add eggs to turkey mixture and mix with hands.

Once bacon is out of the oven, set bacon aside and drain fat into separate container. Form 20 meatballs and lay over the same sheet the bacon cooked on.

Cook meatballs for 15-20 minutes or until juices run clear, then skewer 2-3 pieces of bacon to each meatball. Each meatball should get 1/2 a piece of bacon.

In the food processor, combine spinach, reserved bacon fat, and seasonings of your choosing. Form mixture into a cylinder, cut into slices and serve under meatballs.
