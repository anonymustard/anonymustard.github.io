Arroz Caldo (Filipino Chicken and Rice Soup)

"Staples", Chicken, Dinner, Gluten Free, InstantPot, Meat, rice, Soup

Prep 25 minutes Cook 25 minutes Makes 6 Source Seriouseats.com

Ingredients

  • Toppings:
  • 1/2 cup canola oil
  • 8 cloves garlic, sliced
  • chicken skin from 2 lbs chicken thighs, chopped
  • 2 hard-boiled eggs, sliced
  • 2 fresh key limes, quartered
  • 4 scallions, sliced
  • hot sauce

  • Soup:
  • 3 tablespoons canola oil
  • 1 medium onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 tablespoon fresh ginger, minced
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon Asian fish sauce
  • 1 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper, plus more to taste
  • 1 cup uncooked jasmine rice
  • 6 cups chicken stock or low-sodium broth
  • 2 pounds chicken thighs
  • 1 tablespoon key lime juice
  • salt

Directions

1. For toppings, heat oil at medium high until approximately 300 degrees Fahrenheit. Fry garlic and chicken skins in batches until golden brown and crispy, about 5 minutes per batch. Strain and drain on paper towels. Serve the eggs, limes, and scallions cold or at room temperature.

2. Turn on InstantPot's saute function. Heat oil in InstantPot until hot. Add onion and cook, stirring occasionally, until softened, but not browned, about 5 minutes. Add ginger and minced garlic and cook for 1 minute longer. Stir in fish sauce and pepper and cook for 1 minute. Add rice and stir until well coated.

3. Stir in chicken stock, running a spoon along the bottom of the InstantPot to release any stuck browned bits. Add chicken thighs and push down under chicken stock and rice. Set InstantPot to Manual mode, High pressure, and cook for 4 minutes. Let release naturally for 10 minutes, then quick release using the vent.

4. Remove the chicken and cut out any bones. Chop chicken into bite-sized pieces and stir into InstantPot.

5. Stir in 1 tablespoon lime juice and salt and pepper to taste. Ladle soup into bowls. Top with garlic, chicken skins, scallions, eggs, lime juice, and hot sauce.
