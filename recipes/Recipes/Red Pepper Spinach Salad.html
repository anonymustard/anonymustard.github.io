Red Pepper Spinach Salad

keto, rice, salad, Sides

Makes 4 servings Source Ruled.me

Ingredients

  • 6 cups spinach
  • ¼ cup ranch dressing
  • 3 tablespoons Parmesan cheese
  • 1 teaspoon red pepper flakes

Directions

Add spinach to a mixing bowl, then drench in ranch.

Mix everything together and add your parmesan and red pepper flakes.

Mix everything together again and serve.

Nutrition

Per serving, you are looking at: 212.86 Calories, 19.49g Fats, 2.64g Net Carbs, and 6.5g Protein.
