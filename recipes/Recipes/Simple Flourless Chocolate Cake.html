Simple Flourless Chocolate Cake

chocolate, Dessert, Gluten Free

Prep 15 minutes Cook 30 minutes Makes 16 Source 101cookingfortwo.com

Ingredients

  • Cake:
  • 7.5 ounces semi-sweet chocolate chips good quality
  • 5 ounces unsalted butter
  • 1/4 teaspoons salt
  • 1 1/4 teaspoon vanilla
  • 187 grams sugar
  • 4 large eggs
  • 54 grams dutch cocoa powder sifted

  • Glaze:
  • 7.5 ounces semi-sweet chocolate chips
  • 5/8 cups cream

Directions

Preheat oven to 375.

Coat the side of a 9-inch cake pan with butter and dust with cocoa powder. Don't bother with the bottom. Place parchment in the bottom of the pan.

Sift cocoa powder. If you skip this, your cake may be a bit grainy.

Over a double boiler, melt chocolate chips with butter. While melting, add vanilla and salt. Stir frequently until smooth. About 5 minutes.

Remove from heat, add sugar and mix well until not grainy. Add eggs and mix well and finally add sifted cocoa powder and whisk until smooth.

Pour into prepared pan and bake until internal temperature of 200 or a little more. 22 minutes for me with a dark pan. It may be up to 25 minutes for a different pan. Please go by the temperature.

Remove from oven and allow to cool in pan for 5-10 minutes. Loosen the sides with a spatula or dull knife. Place your serving dish on the pan and flip over onto the plate. The bottom will be up, but that is ok. Remove parchment paper.

Allow to cool to room temperature.

For the glaze, using a larger non-stick saucepan over very low heat, melt chocolate chips and cream. Milk should be OK here, but use cream if available.

Pour into the center of the cake and spread to the edge smoothly. Some will drizzle down the side and look great. Cool for several hours before serving.

Notes

2016-12-31: Adapted for 9 inch cake pan

2017-01-01: Accidentally doubled amount of cream in glaze. It was thin, and I couldn't pour it all on the cake. It eventually cooled to a thick syrup instead of a hard shell. A nice accident!

This cake is extremely rich, and you should serve small slices. Try topped with berries and whipped cream.
