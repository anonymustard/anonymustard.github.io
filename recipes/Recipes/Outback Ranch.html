Outback Ranch

Uncategorized

Prep 5 min Cook 20mins Makes Serves: 75 Source geniuskitchen.com

Ingredients

  • 64 cups mayonnaise
  • 32 cups buttermilk
  • 18 teaspoons table salt
  • 13 1/2 teaspoons cafe ground black pepper
  • 13 1/2 teaspoons onion powder
  • 13 1/2 teaspoons garlic powder
  • 4 1/2 teaspoons cayenne pepper

Directions

Add buttermilk and spices.

Mix well.

Add mayo and then mix on low 15 minutes.

Stays fresh 3-5 days.
