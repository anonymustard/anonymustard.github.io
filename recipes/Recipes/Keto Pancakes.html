Keto Pancakes

breakfast, Gluten Free, keto

Makes 8 pancakes Source The Keto Cookbook

Ingredients

  • 4 oz cream cheese, softened
  • 4 large eggs
  • 2 teaspoon sweetener (Erythritol, Stevia, Splenda)
  • 1 tablespoon vanilla extract
  • dash cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon maple extract (optional)
  • 28 grams coconut flour
  • 2 teaspoon baking powder
  • almond milk

Directions

Mix cream cheese, eggs, sweetener, vanilla, cinnamon, and maple extract in stand mixer with paddle attachment.

Mix in coconut flour and baking powder. Add almond milk if batter is too thick.

Heat pan to medium heat and spray with cooking oil. Portion out 4 tablespoons of batter per pancake. Cook 2-3 minutes per side until golden brown.

Nutrition

2 grams net carbs per pancake
