Lmnt's Chili

"Staples", Beef, Chicken, Dinner, Full meal, Gluten Free, InstantPot, Meat, Soup

Source Pepperplate.com

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds ground meat (beef, chicken, turkey, pork, lamb, sausage, and/or chorizo)
  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 3 jalapenos, chopped
  • 1 green pepper, chopped
  • 7 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried parsley
  • 1 tablespoon chili powder
  • 1 tablespoon whole cumin seeds
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 14.5 oz cans diced tomatoes, drained
  • 1 can kidney beans, drained, rinsed
  • 1 can great northern beans, drained, rinsed, mashed into paste
  • 6 ounces tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon cilantro
  • 14.5 ounces chicken broth
  • to taste: black pepper, salt, chili powder, hot sauce

Directions

Brown meat in batches; drain, reserving some grease to saute vegetables; add to 1 gallon pot

Cook onions, peppers, and garlic in skillet with oil or grease until onions are soft; add to pot

Add herbs, spices, beans, and tomatoes to pot

Add chicken broth to pot; stir; bring to boil

Cook: (a) slow cooker: 3-12 hours; (b) stove: simmer for 3 hours; or (c) pressure cooker: 1 hour

Season to taste
