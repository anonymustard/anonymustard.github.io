Curry Sweet Potatoes With Spicy Yogurt Sauce

"Staples", Indian, potato, Sides, Starch

Ingredients

  • SAUCE:
  • 1 cup yogurt or Greek yogurt
  • 1 Tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 tsp minced garlic
  • 1/2 tsp paprika
  • 1/2 tsp cayenne pepper
  • 1/2 tsp onion powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • POTATOES:
  • 2 lbs sweet potatoes, cut into wedges
  • 2 Tbsp oil
  • 4 tsp curry powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp pepper

Directions

Sauce:

Mix all ingredients and chill

Potatoes:

Toss potatoes with other ingredients

Spread on greased cookie sheet and roast at 450 degrees for 30 minutes
