José Taco

"Staples", Beef, Dinner, Tex-Mex

Source Inspired by Good Eats: American Classics VIII: Tacos

Ingredients

  • Seasoning:
  • 1 tablespoons chili powder
  • 1/2 tablespoon ground cumin
  • 2 teaspoons cornstarch
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons hot smoked paprika
  • 1 teaspoon ground coriander
  • 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • 1 tablespoon onion powder

  • mild Seasoning:
  • 1 tablespoons chili powder
  • 3/4 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 2 teaspoons cornstarch
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons hot smoked paprika
  • 1 teaspoon ground coriander
  • 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • 1 tablespoon onion powder

  • Taco meat:
  • 2 tablespoons canola oil
  • 1 pound ground sirloin
  • 2 gloves garlic, minced
  • 2/3 teaspoon Better than Bouillon Beef Base
  • 2/3 cup water
  • Seasoning mixture


Directions

Seasoning: Put all of the ingredients in a small jar and shake to combine. Store in an airtight container for up to 1 month.

Taco meat:

Add oil to skillet over medium heat. Wait until oil starts to shimmer.

Add ground sirloin and garlic to skillet. Cook until meat is browned, about 3-4 minutes

Add beef base, water, and entire seasoning mixture. Stir and bring to a simmer. Cook uncovered until sauce thickens, about 2-3 minutes
