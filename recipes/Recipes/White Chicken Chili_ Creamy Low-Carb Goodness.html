White Chicken Chili: Creamy Low-Carb Goodness

Chicken, Full meal, Gluten Free, InstantPot, keto, large batch, Meat, Tex-Mex

Prep 5 mins Cook 8 hr Makes Servings: 6 Source Lowcarb-ology.com

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds chicken breasts or thighs, cut into 1 inch pieces
  • 2 teaspoons chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • ½ cup chicken stock
  • 9 ounces chopped green chiles drained (2 4.5-ounce cans)
  • 100 grams onions diced
  • 3 tablespoons butter
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 4 ounces cream cheese
  • 1/2 cup sour cream
  • 1/2 tsp xanthan gum
  • Toppings:
  • 1/3 cup chopped cilantro if desired
  • 1/3 cup red onion peeled and chopped
  • 1 cup shredded Pepper-Jack cheese

Directions

Add 1 tbsp butter to pot. Sauté onions in butter. Add spices and mix.

Add chicken stock, and green chiles.

Cook on high pressure for 15 minutes.

heat the butter, cream, cream cheese, and sour cream in a saucepan, until melted and smooth.

Add the cream mixture to the chicken mixture in the pressure cooker and cook on high pressure for 15 minutes.

Add xanthan gum and heat uncovered until thick.

Sprinkle with cilantro, red onion, and Pepper Jack to serve.
