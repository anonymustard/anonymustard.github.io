Kung Pao chicken stir fry

"Staples", Chicken, Full meal, keto, Meat

Makes 2

Ingredients

  • Chicken:
  • 1 lb chicken thighs or breasts, ground or cubed
  • Salt and pepper
  • 1 tsp ground ginger
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp sambal oeleek
  • 1 1/2 tbsp cooking oil

  • Sauce:
  • 1 tbsp liquid aminos or soy sauce
  • 1 tsp rice vinegar
  • 1 tbsp minced garlic
  • 1 tbsp sambal oeleek
  • 1 tbsp reduced sugar ketchup
  • 2 tsp toasted sesame oil
  • 1 tbsp peanut butter
  • 1/2 tsp maple extract
  • 10 drops liquid stevia extract

Directions

1. Heat oil in a large saucepan over medium to medium-high heat. Add chicken and season with salt and pepper. Cook until chicken is done. Add ginger, garlic, and sambal oeleek near end of cooking. Transfer cooked chicken to bowl.

2. Mix together sauce components

3. Have each diner select stir fry ingredients, including onions, bell peppers, carrots, broccoli, bean sprouts, water chestnuts. Add them to a bowl along with a serving of chicken.

4. Stir fry each bowl with oil at medium-high heat. Add half of sauce near end of cooking and stir constantly to coat and thicken. Remove from heat and serve immediately.
