Paleo Brussels Sprout Salad

keto, salad, Sides, Vegetarian

Makes Serves 4 Source Americastestkitchen.com

Ingredients

  • 3 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 1 small shallot, minced
  • 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard
  • 1 garlic clove, minced
  • Kosher salt and pepper
  • 1 pound Brussels sprouts, trimmed, halved, and sliced very thin
  • ½ cup pine nuts, toasted

Directions

Slicing the sprouts as thin as possible is important to the success of this dish.

1. Whisk oil, lemon juice, shallot, mustard, garlic, and 1 teaspoon salt together in large serving bowl. Add Brussels sprouts and toss to coat. Let salad sit for at least 30 minutes or up to 2 hours.

2. Stir pine nuts into salad and season with salt and pepper to taste. Serve.
