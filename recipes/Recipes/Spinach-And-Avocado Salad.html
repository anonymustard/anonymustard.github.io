Spinach-And-Avocado Salad

keto, salad, Sides

Makes 4 servings Source Myrecipes.com

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 tablespoons fresh lime juice
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1/2 tablespoon chopped fresh cilantro
  • 4 drops stevia extract
  • 1/8 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1/8 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 1/8 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/2 Hass avocado, peeled, pitted and thinly sliced
  • 1/2 small red onion or 2-3 stalks celery, thinly sliced
  • 6 ounces baby spinach

Directions

Whisk lime juice, oil, cilantro, sugar, cumin, salt and pepper in a large serving bowl. Stir in avocado and red onion or celery. Lay spinach on top. (Salad can be prepared and refrigerated up to 2 hours ahead.) Toss just before serving.
