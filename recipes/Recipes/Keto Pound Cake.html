Keto Pound Cake

Dessert, keto

Makes Yields 16 servings Source Ruled.me

Ingredients

  • Pound cake:
  • 4 ounces unsalted butter, softened
  • 8 ounces cream cheese, softened
  • 288 grams erythritol
  • 8 whole eggs, room temperature
  • 1 ½ teaspoons vanilla extract
  • ½ teaspoon lemon extract
  • 280 grams almond flour
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • 1 ½ teaspoons baking powder
  • Glaze:
  • 48 grams powdered erythritol
  • 3 tablespoons heavy whipping cream
  • ½ teaspoon mix of vanilla extract, orange extract, lemon extract, almond extract, etc.

Directions

The Execution

Preheat oven to 350°F. In a large mixing bowl, toss in room temperature butter, softened cream cheese and erythritol.

Cream together the butter, cream cheese and erythritol and until smooth.

Add in the eggs, lemon extract, and vanilla extract in with the blended ingredients. Blend with a hand mixer until smooth.

In a medium sized bowl, mix together the almond flour, baking powder, and salt.

Slowly add the dry ingredients to the liquid ingredients. Use a hand blender to blend the clumps until very smooth.

Pour batter into a loaf pan. Bake for 60 - 120 minutes at 350F or until smooth in the middle with a toothpick.

OR: Pour batter in to cupcake pans. Bake for 20-45 minutes at 350F

If creating a glaze, blend together the powdered erythritol, extracts and heavy whipping cream until smooth. Wait until the pound cake is fully cooled from the oven before spreading the glaze on top.
