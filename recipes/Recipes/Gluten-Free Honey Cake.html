Gluten-Free Honey Cake

Dessert, Gluten Free, Holiday

Source pepperplate.com

Ingredients

  • 180 grams brown rice flour
  • 216 grams potato starch
  • 120 grams tapioca flour
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 1/2 teaspoon xanthan gum
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 152 grams turbinado sugar or brown sugar
  • 4 eggs, separated
  • 12 ounces honey
  • 1/4 cup vegetable oil
  • 4 tablespoons applesauce
  • 1 cup orange juice (about 5 oranges)
  • 2 teaspoons grated orange zest (about 2 oranges)

Directions

1. Mix flours, baking powder, baking soda, xanthan gum, nutmeg, cinnamon, and salt

2. Combine sugar, egg yolks, honey, oil, applesauce, orange juice, and orange zest

3. Add dry ingredients to wet ingredients and mix to combine

4. Beat egg whites to form stiff peaks and fold in to mixture

5. Pour batter into pans and bake at 350 F for 40 minutes (round cakes) or 45-50 minutes (loaves and bundt pans)

6. Drizzle cakes with honey before serving
