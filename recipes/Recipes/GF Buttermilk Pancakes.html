GF Buttermilk Pancakes

Uncategorized

Makes 16 3-inch pancakes Source How can it be Gluten Free?

Ingredients

  • 169 g white rice flour
  • 53 g brown rice flour
  • 49 g potato starch
  • 85 g tapioca starch
  • 5.3 g dehydrated milk powder
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1 3/4 cup buttermilk
  • 2 large eggs, separated
  • 4 tbsp unsalted butter, melted and cooled
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 1-2 tsp vegetable oil

Directions

Adjust oven rack to middle position and heat oven to 200 degrees. Spray wire rack set inside rimmed baking sheet with oil spray; place in oven.

Whisk flours, starches, milk powder, salt, baking powder, and baking soda in a large bowl.

In a separate bowl, whisk buttermilk, egg yolks, and melted butter.

Whip egg whites on medium-low speed until foamy, about 1 minute. Increase speed to medium-high and whip whites to soft billowy mounds, about 1 minute. Gradually add sugar and whip until gloss stiff peaks form, 2-3 minutes.

Whisk buttermilk mixture into flour mixture until thickened and nolumps remain. Fold in whipped egg whites until just combined and few streaks remain.

Heat 1 tsp oil in 12-inch nonstick skillet over medium heat until shimmering, 3-5 minutes. Using paper towels, carefully wipe out oil, leaving a thin film on the bottom and sides of pan.

Use 1/4 cup of batter per pancake. Portion into skillet and cook until bottoms are brown and top surfaces start to bubble, 2-3 minutes. Flip and cook until second side has browned, 1-2 minutes longer.

Transfer pancakes to wire rack in oven to keep warm while cooking remaining pancakes.
