Easy Oven Baked Parmesan Crusted Tilapia

fish, Meat

Prep 4 servings 10 minutes Cook 12 minutes Makes 4 servings Source 101cookingfortwo.com

Ingredients

  • 1 lb Tilapia
  • 1 cup Parmesan cheese grated fresh if possible
  • 1 tablespoon paprika
  • 1 tablespoon dried parsley
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1/2 teaspoons salt
  • 1/2 teaspoons pepper

Directions

Preheat oven to 400 degrees convection. Line a baking sheet with aluminum foil.

Wash and pat dry tilapia.

Mix parmesan, paprika, parsley, salt and pepper in a shallow pan.

Brush tilapia with oil on both sides and then dredge in the parmesan mixture patting the coating on. Place on the baking sheet.

Take remaining parmesan mixture an pat on top of the already coated fish.

Bake for about 12 minutes until flaky.
