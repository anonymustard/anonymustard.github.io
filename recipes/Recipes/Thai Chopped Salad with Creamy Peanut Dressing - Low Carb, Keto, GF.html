Thai Chopped Salad with Creamy Peanut Dressing - Low Carb, Keto, GF

Uncategorized

Makes Yield - 4 servings Source Modernlowcarb.com

Ingredients

  • 8 cups romaine lettuce
  • 2 skinless, boneless chicken breast
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp minced ginger
  • 1/2 cup chopped cilantro
  • 1 carrot, shredded
  • 1/4 cup peanuts
  • 1/4 cup cucumber, julienned
  • 2 tbsp chopped green onion
  • 2 tbsp peanut butter, natural, no sugar added
  • 2 tbsp rice vinegar
  • 3 tbsp avocado oil (or your choice of oil)
  • 2 tsp sesame oil
  • 1/2 tsp tamari (gluten-free soy sauce substitute)

Directions

In a skillet over medium heat, add minced garlic clove and ginger to 1 tbsp oil. Cook for 1-2 minutes until fragrant, taking care not to burn.

Add chicken breasts and cook for approximately 4 minutes on each side, until all pink has disappeared. Set aside to cool and then slice into 1/2 strips.

To a large salad bowl, add lettuce, cucumber, green onion, shredded carrot and chopped cilantro. Top with chicken strips.

In a small bowl, whisk together peanut butter, vinegar, 2 tbsp avocado oil, sesame oil and tamari.

Drizzle dressing on top of salad and add peanuts to top. Serve and enjoy!

Nutrition

Per Serving: 380 calories, 31 grams fat, 8 grams carbohydrate, 3.5 grams dietary fiber, 4.5 grams net carbohydrate, 26 grams protein.
