Ginger marinated tuna steaks

"Staples", fish, keto

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1/2 tbsp sesame oil
  • 1/2 tbsp rice vinegar
  • 1 tsp minced garlic
  • 1 tsp minced ginger
  • 1 tbsp chopped onions
  • black pepper
  • 2 tuna steaks (8 oz each)

Directions

Combine soy sauce, sesame oil, vinegar, garlic, ginger, and onions in a 1 quart zip top bag. Add tuna steaks and seal bag, removing all air. Squish contents of bag together so both tuna steaks are covered in marinade. Marinate for 30-60 minutes, turning from time to time.

Pan-fry steaks on non-stick skillet or grill about 8 minutes until done, flipping once.
