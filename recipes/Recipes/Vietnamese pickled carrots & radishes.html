Vietnamese pickled carrots & radishes

Gluten Free, salad, Sides, Vegetarian

Makes 3 cups

Ingredients

  • To dry:
  • 1/2 lb carrots, peeled and cut into matchsticks
  • 1 1/2 lb daikon radish, peeled and cut into matchsticks
  • 2 tsp kosher salt
  • 1 tsp sugar

  • To brine:
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup warm water
  • 6-8 tbsp rice vinegar

Directions

Dry the vegetables: Combine carrots, salt, and sugar. Mix with hands, rubbing into vegetables. Let sit in bowl until vegetables have wilted and liquid pools at the bottom, about 30 minutes. Drain, rinse, and pat dry.

Brine: Whisk together remaining sugar, vinegar, and water and pour over vegetables. Stir to combine. Set aside to marinate for at least 1 hour, or refrigerate tightly covered for up to 4 weeks.
