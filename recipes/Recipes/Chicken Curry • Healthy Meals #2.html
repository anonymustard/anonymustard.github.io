Chicken Curry • Healthy Meals #2

"Staples", Chicken, Full meal, keto, Meat

Source Iamkika.com

Ingredients

  • 2 cups (500 grams) of skinless chicken thighs, diced
  • 1 tablespoon of curry powder
  • 2 teaspoons of cumin powder
  • 2 teaspoons of coriander powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon of cayenne pepper
  • 1/3 cup of tomato paste
  • 1/2 medium onion, chopped
  • 1/4 cup of olive oil
  • 2 cans (540ml) of coconut milk
  • 1 cup (200 grams) of broccoli, chopped
  • 1 cup (200 grams) of green beans
  • Salt and black pepper to taste

Directions

In a bowl mix chicken thighs, curry, cumin and coriander powder, cayenne pepper, and tomato paste.

Let it rest for 1 hour in the fridge.

Later, in a pot add olive oil and marinated chicken, cooking until the chicken has a nice golden colour.

Then add onions and cook until tender.

Next add coconut milk and let it simmer for 10 minutes.

Finally, add green beans, broccoli and season with salt and black pepper to taste.

Let it simmer for another 10 minutes.

IT’S DONE!

Serve it with regular rice or a yummy cauliflower rice.
