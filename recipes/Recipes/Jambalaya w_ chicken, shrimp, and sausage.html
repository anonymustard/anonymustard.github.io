Jambalaya w/ chicken, shrimp, and sausage

★★★★★

"Staples", Chicken, Dinner, Full meal, Gluten Free, keto, large batch, Meat, rice

Prep 15 minutes Cook 45 minutes Makes 10 cups

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 4 tablespoons creole spice mix, divided
  • 1 pound boneless skinless chicken thighs, cut into 1" cubes
  • 1 pound Andouille sausage, sliced into half moons
  • 2 celery stalks, diced
  • 1 medium onion, diced
  • 3 green bell peppers, diced
  • 1 jalapeno, diced
  • 3 tablespoons minced garlic
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 1 1/2 cup clam juice and/or chicken stock (any proportion)
  • 14.5 ounce can diced tomatoes, including liquid
  • 2 teaspoons Worcestershire sauce
  • 4 teaspoons Louisiana hot sauce
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1 pound raw shrimp, peeled and deveined

  • hot sauce
  • cooked rice

Directions

Toss chicken with 1 tablespoon creole seasoning. In a stock pot, cook chicken in oil until browned on all sides. Add sausage and cook for 3-5 minutes.

Add celery, onion, peppers, and jalapeno and cook for 5 minutes, stirring constantly, until onion is translucent. Add garlic and stir until garlic is fragrant, 30 seconds.

Add remaining 3 tablespoons creole seasoning and tomato paste. Stir until combined and cook 1 minute.

Add clam juice or chicken stock, diced tomatoes, Worcestershire sauce, hot sauce, and bay leaf. Mix well. Bring to a boil, cover, reduce heat, and simmer for 20 minutes.

Add raw shrimp to pot, stir, cover, and simmer for 5-10 minutes until shrimp is cooked.

Remove from heat and let sit uncovered for 10 minutes to thicken.

Serve with hot sauce and rice.
